ember has been deployed!

to connect from within the cluster:

  redis-cli -h {{ include "ember.fullname" . }}.{{ .Release.Namespace }}.svc.cluster.local -p {{ .Values.service.port }}

to connect from your local machine:

  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "ember.fullname" . }} {{ .Values.service.port }}:{{ .Values.service.port }}
  redis-cli -h 127.0.0.1 -p {{ .Values.service.port }}
{{- if .Values.ember.metricsPort }}

prometheus metrics are available at:

  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "ember.fullname" . }} {{ .Values.ember.metricsPort }}:{{ .Values.ember.metricsPort }}
  curl http://127.0.0.1:{{ .Values.ember.metricsPort }}/metrics
{{- end }}
